Welcome to AASHROY

A database for homeless.....

    Our country consists of both rich and poor people. Rich people are becoming richer, and poor are becoming homeless. Those people are taking shelter under bridge, footpath and slum areas. Even the Government doesn’t have any proper survey about those people. When Government releases funds for them, the system stomach the money. These people are prone to trafficking, drug abuse and other crimes. So, these people haven’t got a chance to grow. They are basically the neglected part of the society. In order to help these people to get their rights, we have Designed a web portal to ensure proper healthcare facilities and other aids especially to the homeless having physical or mental ailments by providing information about their temporary shelters to the government and volunteer organizations. This is because “AASHROY believes in Lotus blooming from mud."
